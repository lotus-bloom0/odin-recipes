Making a Lasagna

For those that have never made lasagna before, you may have no idea what you need, or where to start. If you have, then you know that it's take a lot of time and effort to put one together. However, there is a reason that so many make the time to put one together.

Today we will sharing with you, the best lasagna you will ever have... probably. All the ingredients you need, as well as detailed step-by-step instructions are waiting below.

  • Two 6 ounce cans of tomato paste
  • ½ cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1½, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
